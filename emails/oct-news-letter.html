About Control

Have you ever felt so overwhelmed by the stress of your daily life just to take a step back and realize that none of it really matters? That's what "Control" is about. We spend so much time, especially in relationships, trying to perfect ourselves when, in reality, things become much easier when we let go. I'm fascinated by the idea that you have more power over your own life if you learn how to relinquish control of things that are inconsequential. Of course, it's much easier said than done, but it's and idea I like to keep floating around in the back of my mind.
